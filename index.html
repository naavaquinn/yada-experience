The Power of Mentorship

More than 1 in 3 young people grow up without a mentor and according to the Youth Purpose Study by William Damon and colleagues:

10%

Of middle school students exhibit a purpose driven orientation

20%

of high school students begin discovering their sense of purpose

40%

of adults report having found a clear sense of purpose.

And adults who were mentored as youth report long-lasting impact...

74%

say their mentor contributed significantly to their success later in life

69%

say that this key relationship helped them with education

58%

say their mentor supported their mental health

Our Story

"YADA" is a Hebrew word that means “to know”

Yada experience was born from a simple but powerful realization that too many young people are navigating life without a clear sense of identity and direction.Through that many wrong choices are made; from wrong career paths to the relationships, we build.

We exist to bridge that gap through creating transformative spaces with meaningful experiences, honest conversations and a supportive community to reflect and reconnect with who you are and why you are here.
