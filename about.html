About YADA-EXPERIENCE

Discover who we are and why we're passionate about purpose discovery

Our Story

Yada is a Hebrew word that means “to know”

Yada experience was born from a simple but powerful realization that too many young people are navigating life without a clear sense of identity and direction.Through that many wrong choices are made; from wrong career paths to the relationships, we build.

We exist to bridge that gap through creating transformative spaces with meaningful experiences, honest conversations and a supportive community to reflect and reconnect with who you are and why you are here.

Our Vision

Raising a generation of bold, purpose-driven visionaries, fueled by passion to transform their world.

Our Mission

Empowering the youth to discover and live their purpose, unleash their strengths through real experiences, growth, and meaningful connections.

Our Values

Respect

Valuing uniqueness and treating all with dignity. Every young person has inherent worth and potential that deserves to be honored and nurtured.

Collaboration

Working together to achieve shared goals and greater impact. We believe in the power of community and collective wisdom.

Integrity

Leading with honesty, accountability, and consistency. We model authentic living we encourage in the youth we serve.

Lifelong Learning

Committed to growth through continuous learning. Curiosity and openness enhance our ability to serve youth effectively.
